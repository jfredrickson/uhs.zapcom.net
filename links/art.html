World of Escher - A Website dedicated to M.C. Escher, who is popular for his spatial illusions, tessellations, and impossible structures. The World of Escher tells stories and provides insight into Escher's work.

The WebMuseum Network - The WebMuseum network is a collection of artwork on the Web. The WebMuseum is a collaborative work of its visitors contributing to improve and expand this online resource.
