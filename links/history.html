US Historical Documents - A collection of documents from United States history, such as the Declaration of Independence, World War II related documents, treaties, and so on, from the Wiretap Online Library.

THOMAS - A collection of legislative information on the Web. THOMAS was brought online under the directive of the leadership of the 104th Congress to make federal legislative information freely available to the Internet public.

Congressional Picture Directory - Pictures of government officials, including the President, Senators, and Representatives.

The United Nations - The United Nations is an organization of sovereign nations. It provides the machinery to help find solutions to disputes or problems, and to deal with virtually any matter of concern to humanity.

Civil War Page - Many resources, links, and helpful information on the American Civil War.

Exploring Ancient World Cultures - EAWC is an introductory, on-line, college-level "textbook" of ancient world cultures.

SCORE History/Social Science - A page designed specifically for California students and teachers, consisting of history and social science resources and links. It is one of several Schools of California On-line Resources for Education (SCORE) pages.
