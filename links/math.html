Ask Dr. Math - A program where Swarthmore College math students answer math-related questions from other students. All questions and answers to problems are added to the Web archive which can be conveniently browsed. Math questions include these anywhere between elementary school problems to college level problems and beyond.

MathMagic - MathMagic is a K-12 telecommunications project developed in El Paso, Texas. It provides strong motivation for students to use computer technology while increasing problem-solving strategies and communications skills. MathMagic posts challenges in each of four categories (k-3, 4-6, 7-9 and 10-12) to trigger each registered team to pair up with another team and engage in a problem-solving dialog. Anyone with Internet access can be involved.

The Geometry Center - A mathematics research and education center at the University of Minnesota, funded by the National Science Foundation as part of the Science and Technology Center program. Useful information and interactive Web applications related to geometry can be found here.

SCORE Mathematics - A page designed specifically for California students and teachers, consisting of math-related resources and links. It is one of several Schools of California On-line Resources for Education (SCORE) pages.
