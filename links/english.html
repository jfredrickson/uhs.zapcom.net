Luminarium - A wonderful repository of information on Middle Ages, Renaissance, and 17th Century English literature! From here, you can find anthologies, author biographies, additional sources, analyses of stories, the stories themselves from the three periods, and more!

Project Bartleby - Created by the Bartleby Library at Columbia University, this project puts literature online for educational use by the public.

Roget's Thesaurus - By Dr. Peter Mark Roget. A catalog of English words and phrases that can be accessed by keyword searching or by browsing an outline which groups words by similarity of meaning.

Webster's Dictionary - A hypertext interface to Webster's Dictionary.

The Internet Public Library - The first public library of the Internet. The IPL provides library services to the Internet community.

The On-line Books Page - An index of thousands of on-line books, and common repositories of on-line books and other documents.

Shakespeare - A complete collection of all of William Shakespeare's works.

SCORE Language Arts - A page designed specifically for California students and teachers, consisting of language arts related resources and links. It is one of several Schools of California On-line Resources for Education (SCORE) pages.
