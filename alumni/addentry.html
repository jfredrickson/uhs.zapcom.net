Alumni Registration

To be added to the Ukiahi alumni page, please fill out this form.

If you don't have an e-mail address or a homepage, just leave them blank. Anything you type in the "Comments" area will be put next to your entry on the alumni page, so you can use it to let friends know how you're doing now. In the "Password" box, type any password you want. This will be used so that in the future, if you would like to update your info on the alumni page, we can confirm that it is you. Do not use the same password you use for your Internet account!

Note that the only required fields are your name, year graduated, and password.

If you would like to update your entry, then just fill out this form (don't forget your password, you need it for validation!) and your entry will be updated.

After you submit this form, it might take a day or two for your listing to show up. If your submission still hasn't appeared in a week, e-mail us and let us know.
