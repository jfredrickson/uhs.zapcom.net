About Ukiah High School

Ukiah High School, covering 60 acres of land in Ukiah, is part of the Ukiah Unified School District. Over 1,800 students are enrolled and there are 63 teachers and staff, making it the largest school in the district. Ukiah High School is a 1988 California Distinguished School.
