Help with the navigation imagemap

At the top of every page (except for the main UHS page) you'll see a series of three small images. Clicking on one image will bring you to a helpful part of the Website.


The image of a house, or home, brings you to the "home" page of UHS. Pretty clever, eh? Too bad we didn't invent the idea!


The magnifying glass image will bring you to the search page where you can search for certain words or phrases on this Website without having to go through all the pages yourself.


The help icon brings you here, and this page should be self-explanatory. :-)

Lost?

If you can't find what you're looking for, you might want to try the UHS Website Search or the Site Index. You might also want to try one of these excellent Web search engines: Alta Vista, Yahoo!, WebCrawler, or for software: CNET's shareware.com.
